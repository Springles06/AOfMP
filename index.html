Welcome to AOMP

Welcome to the Art of Mathematical Problems (AOMP), a vibrant community of Saudi Arabian Math Olympiad enthusiasts. Our mission is to foster a love for mathematics and problem-solving among students in Saudi Arabia.

Whether you are a beginner looking to explore the fascinating world of mathematics or an advanced learner aiming to hone your skills, AOMP has something to offer for everyone. Join us as we delve deep into the world of mathematical problems and discover the joy of learning.

What We Offer

Our website features:

  • AOMP Lessons - Our handcrafted problem sets are designed to challenge and inspire students. These lessons cover various topics including geometry, number theory, algebra, and combinatorics.
  • Camp Materials - Get access to a rich collection of materials from past math camps. These resources include problem sets, solutions, e-training modules, and online lectures.
  • Useful Links - We have curated a list of websites and resources that can be instrumental in your mathematical olympiad journey. From official Olympiad websites to personal blogs of renowned mathematicians, find it all here.
