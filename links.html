Useful Links

In this section, we have curated a list of external resources that can be a great aid in your mathematical journey. These links provide a wealth of information, ranging from official Olympiad websites to personal blogs of renowned mathematicians.

We encourage you to explore these resources to further enhance your understanding and appreciation of mathematics. Happy learning!

  • Art of Problem Solving - A comprehensive platform offering online classes, books, and a vibrant community of math enthusiasts.
  • A collection of Olympiad Books - A curated list of books that are highly recommended for Olympiad preparation.
  • Evan Chen's Personal Blog - A personal blog by Evan Chen, a well-known figure in the mathematical community, where he shares insights and resources on various mathematical topics.
